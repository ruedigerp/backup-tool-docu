Interested Parties: Stakeholders and their requirements¶

Stakeholders¶

Stakeholder Impact Requirements
Banks medium - Consistent income from financial transactions
- Long term relationship
Society low - Environmental awareness
- Integrity
Competitors low - Fair treatment
Federal Employment Agency mediam - Imparting of technical knowledge
- Compliance with the approved measure
- Placement of graduates
Media medium - Information/transparency (e.g. in case of security incidents)
Management high - Profit optimization
- Cost efficiency
- Strategic development
Owners/shareholders medium - Profit
- Reducing liability risks
- Power, influence, prestige
- Meeting of financial and strategic goals
Applicants medium - Protection of personal rights
- Employer of choice: Positive feedback on pen platforms such as kununu
- Gender equality
Clients/customers high - Punctual delivery
- Confidential handling of sensitive information
- Reliability/availability
Service providers/suppliers high - Punctual payment
- Integrity, Availability, Confidentiality
Chief Technology Officer high - Reliability/availability
- Implement projects on time
- Limited additional effort due to information security (resources/capacities)
- Budgets (Who covers the costs for necessary measures?)
Employees high - Salary payment (punctual, regular)
- Fair treatment
- Access to necessary tools, information and hardware in order to fulfil their tasks
Legislators/authorities high - Taxes
- Environmental protection
- Job security
- Legal compliance

Review¶

This overview is reviewed and updated annually and as required.
